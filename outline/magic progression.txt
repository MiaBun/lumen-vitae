 Tier 1: Nature and Spirit

Theme: The awakening of natural magic — the first contact with mana, spirits, and the living essence of the world.
Goal: Learn basic mana manipulation, druidic energy, and environmental magic.

Core Mods:

 Nature’s Aura – introduces natural energy systems and passive world magic.

 Botania (early game access only) – start with flowers, mana pools, and functional flora.

 Hexerei – witchcraft with herbs, brews, familiars, and broom flight.

 Mermod – introduces mermaid transformation and water magic.

 Ars Nouveau (Beginner tier) – learn basic spellcasting, glyphs, and spell creation.

 GeOre + GeOre Nouveau – natural crystal attunement and early resource gathering.

Dimensions & Exploration:

 The Aether – the first “Good Magic” dimension; where spirits dwell.

 Everbright (Blue Skies) – serves as a natural realm of radiant energies.

Boss Integration:

Aether bosses (e.g., Slider, Valkyrie Queen)

Blue Skies Everbright bosses
(Represent spiritual trials.)

Progression Unlock:

Unlock access to ritual magic and start using mana networks or spirit channels.

May require an Essence of Spirit from Everbright bosses to progress.

 Tier 2: Ritual Initiate

Theme: Structured practice of magic through circles, rituals, and offerings to entities.
Goal: Learn to perform controlled magic through rites, sigils, and intermediaries.

Core Mods:

 Occultism – summoning spirits, performing rituals, and learning about the “other side.”

 Enchanted: Witchcraft – improves witchcraft and ritual circle systems.

 Forbidden and Arcanus – the first taste of dark but restrained magic.

 Hexerei (Expanded) – advanced rituals, broom upgrades, and familiars.

 Mana and Artifice (Apprentice rank) – introduces structured spellcraft and magical materials.

Dimensions:

 Twilight Forest – serves as the forest of hidden ritual knowledge.

 Everbright remains accessible for light rituals.

Boss Integration:

aquamirae

Twilight Forest bosses as “ritual challenges.”

Occultism’s demons or summoned bosses.

Progression Unlock:

Perform a Grand Rite combining light (Everbright) and shadow (Twilight) energy.

Gain access to Arcane study through a ritual portal or artifact.

 Tier 3: Arcane Scholar

Theme: The age of knowledge and academia — discovering runes, sigils, and structured schools of magic.
Goal: Formalize magic into systems and learn the deeper rules behind it.

Core Mods:

 Ars Nouveau (Intermediate) – advanced spell crafting, automation with familiars.

 Mana and Artifice (Adept rank) – multi-component spells and enchanting.

 Magichem – magical chemistry and reactions.

 Apotheosis (Enchantment) – understanding enchantment systems beyond vanilla limits.

 Primal Magick – balancing primal sources (sun, sea, blood, etc.).

Dimensions:

 Twilight Forest (continued) – deeper ruins and libraries.

 Everbright/Everdawn – representing the academic divide: light vs dark schools.

Boss Integration:

Twilight Forest: Hydra, Lich, Ur-Ghast as magical exams.

Blue Skies Everdawn bosses: dark counterpoint trials.

Progression Unlock:

Combine energies from multiple primal sources.

Unlock Alchemical knowledge and forbidden texts.

 Tier 4: Alchemy and Forbidden Power

Theme: The manipulation of creation itself through transmutation, forbidden runes, and alchemical synthesis.
Goal: Bend the rules of nature and magic to your will.

Core Mods:

 Theurgy – core alchemy framework and energy synthesis.

 Magichem (Advanced) – complex magical crafting and life-energy reactions.

 EvilCraft – corruption-based magic, vampirism, and dark essences.

 Goety – demonic spellcraft and necromancy.

 Forbidden and Arcanus (expanded) – arcane artifacts, soul crystals, and forbidden tools.

 Mystical Agriculture + Customization – alchemical resource transmutation.

Dimensions:

 Everdawn (Blue Skies dark realm)

 The Twilight Forest (Deep zones) – corrupted groves and ancient labs.

Boss Integration:

Blue Skies Everdawn bosses

Cataclysm bosses begin appearing as “manifestations of forbidden energy.”

Progression Unlock:

Unlock Soul and Blood research.

Gain access to Blood Magic Tier 1 (Apprentice).

 Tier 5: Blood and Soul

Theme: The deepest personal connection — magic fueled by life force and will.
Goal: Master blood rites, soul crystals, and energy conversion.

Core Mods:

 Blood Magic – main focus, with tiered altars and rituals.

 Malum – magic of spirit, suffering, and soul manipulation.

 Goety (expanded) – dark pacts, necromantic summons.

 Forbidden and Arcanus – soulbound crafting and artifacts.

 Apotheosis (Boss/Blood branch) – artifact upgrades and dungeon loot.

Dimensions:

 The Undergarden – realm of decay and corrupted souls.

 Alex’s Caves – hidden relics and ancient soul containers.

Boss Integration:

Undergarden bosses

Cataclysm: Ignis, The Harbinger

BOMD: Gauntlet bosses as soul trials.

Progression Unlock:

Perform a Soul Ascension ritual.

Gain access to Arcana Ascension research.

 Tier 6: Arcana Ascension

Theme: Uniting all schools of magic — mastery of the arcane laws of creation.
Goal: Channel light and darkness harmoniously, constructing divine artifacts.

Core Mods:

 Primal Magick (final tier) – balance primal sources.

 Mana and Artifice (Master rank) – reality rewriting spells.

 Botania (Endgame) – Gaia Guardian rituals.

 Mahou Tsukai – anime-style ultimate abilities powered by mana pools.

 Irons Spells n Spellbooks – advanced combat and spell fusion.

Dimensions:

 The Aether (Revisited) – divine trials of ascension.

 The Undergarden (Depths) – final confrontation with darkness.

Boss Integration:

Gaia Guardian II

Cataclysm: The Ender Guardian

BOMD: Final Trials bosses

Progression Unlock:

Achieve Arcane Balance → unlock Universal Synthesis tier.

 Tier 7: Universal Synthesis

Theme: The creation of gods — mastery of both creation and entropy.
Goal: Combine all energies, transcend material limits, and forge the “Philosopher’s Core.”

Core Mods:

 Theurgy (Endgame) – matter-energy transmutation.

 Botania (Creative-level automation)

 Magichem (Grand synthesis)

 Mahou Tsukai (Unlimited mana & reality warping)

 Blood Magic (Archmage tier)

 Occultism (Final tier demon lords)

 Apotheosis (Final) – divine artifacts and relic crafting.

Dimensions:

 Fusion of Aether, Everdawn, and Undergarden through custom portal or event.

Cosmic or custom “Arcane Nexus” dimension could be created via datapack.

Boss Integration:

Cataclysm final bosses

Ice and Fire Dragons (Elder tier)

Optional final encounter: “Avatar of Equilibrium” (custom boss).

Endgame Outcome:

You unify all magic forms into Universal Synthesis — a system that bridges divine and forbidden, crafting pure mana matter and rewriting the world.
